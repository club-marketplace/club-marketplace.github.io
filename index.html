Student Led

Unique to only High School, these clubs are all founded, led, and supported by students. Students have opportunities to showcase their leadership skills and creativity through these independent clubs.

Diverse

Ranging from Chess Club to Sailing Club, Hong Kong International School’s high school offers a wide variety of clubs. Students with different interests and passions have more choices to choose from.

With no grade restrictions, High School’s clubs unifies different students of different ages. These clubs bring together people who share the same passion, relentless of the materialistic barriers.
